    Fix: Set wheelchair_boarding to 1 (accessible) or 2 (not accessible) for every stop. A field survey can start with the busiest stops. (A column in stops.txt; your scheduling software likely has it.)

  • Warning36 instances

    36 of 36 trips have no headsign.

    Riders at the stop can't tell which direction a bus is going.

    Fix: Populate trip_headsign to match what the bus displays. (Usually a bulk edit in your scheduling software.)

  • Warning25 instances

    Some stops exist in the feed but no trip ever stops at them.

Beyond the grade

Opportunities that do not change your grade today: fare detail, on-demand service, and deeper accessibility data.

  • 2 route badge(s) pair a color and text color below the WCAG 4.5:1 contrast bar (GRN, BRN).

    Consider: Adjust route_color or route_text_color so each pair clears 4.5:1. Often switching the text between black and white is enough.

  • 103 stop name(s) use abbreviations or symbols a screen reader may mispronounce, with no spoken form set ("Morning Star Rd and Elkhorn Rd S", "Lower Ranch NE", "Twin Creeks-Bluebell SE", and more).

    Consider: Add tts_stop_name with the spoken form, e.g. 'Main Street and Second Avenue', for the affected stops.
