Top things to fix — most rider-affecting first

Fix 01

Set wheelchair_boarding on every stop

Riders who use a wheelchair can't plan a trip when a stop's accessibility is marked unknown; apps show no information at all.

⏱ A column in stops.txt; your scheduling software likely has it.

Fix 02

Set wheelchair_accessible on every trip

Even with accessible stops, riders need to know the vehicle can carry them. Most small-agency fleets are 100% accessible, so this is often a single default.

⏱ Often one default setting in your export.

Fix 03

Remove unused route shapes

Shapes no trip uses are dead weight from the export and usually a sign of stale data the next reviewer has to wade through.

⏱ One setting.
